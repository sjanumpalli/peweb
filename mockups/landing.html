Our Process

How It Works

From waste collection to clean energy in five streamlined steps.

1

Collection

Non-recyclable plastic waste gathered from municipalities and industrial partners.

2

Sorting

Advanced sorting removes contaminants and categorizes plastic by polymer type.

3

Pyrolysis

Thermal decomposition at 400-500 C breaks down plastics into hydrocarbon vapors.

4

Condensation

Vapors are cooled and condensed into liquid fuel, gas, and carbon black residue.

5

Distribution

Clean fuel is tested, certified, and distributed to industries and communities.
